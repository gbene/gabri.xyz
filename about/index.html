About this site

This site is ment to function as a place to organize and archive my projects in a single place. Too many times I’ve started a project and never finish it because I’ve forgot about it or it became too messy to start all over again. By creating this site I hope to build a solid structure as future reference, use it to document progress over time of new ideas and help me reconsider and/or finish suspended projects. The site is written in Go Hugo, an open source static site generator available under the Apache Licence 2.0. Learn more on GitHub.

About me

I’m a 22 years old geology student with a passion for science, technology, woodworking, nature and photography. I was always fascinated by the complexity of our Earth and during my academic course I found (and still find) endless possibilities of applying modern technology and coding to automate tedious tasks and make everything more efficient. It doesn’t help that I love puzzles, dissasembling different objects, fixing stuff and try to understand how different parts and different objects work and can relate. Because of this I love to get my hands dirty by always learing something new (tools, softwares, etc…) and create specific tools to help me understand.
